On Saturday 17 November 2007, the Italian Community of PostgreSQL has finally founded a non profit organisation for the promotion of the open source RDBMS in Italy.

Participation to the association is open to anyone, as stated by the NPO charter (statute).

For more information about PostgreSQL please refer to the official PostgreSQL Website.

This website is primarily intended for an Italian speaking audience.
